Gangs Rules and guidelines:

- Every gang must have a gang leader who has to lead the team and he is responsible for all the gang related activities. If any mistake or rule break happened by a gang member, gang leader should take the responsibility and sort out the issues between both gangs.

- Gang RP is a big subject and everything cannot be framed in rules. So after or before initiating a Gang RP gang leaders should talk in OOC about how they are going to do a Gang RP and If any issues or Fail RP then Two gang leaders should talk in our let's Talk Discord Voice channel and sort out all the issues faced in their gang Rp.

- Gang vengeance should be completely IC only and if any OOC vengeance is seen it is the gang leader's responsibility to sort out that issues with the opposite gang.

- If any OOC vengeance or other major issues is cause by a gang, then admins have the right to suspend the gang activity temporarily or permanently based on the severity.

- Don't raise ticket for silly reason, Gang leaders speak and sort out the issues and continue your gang Rp. If there is a major issue, then you can raise a ticket and contact HH or admins through tickets and wait for them to respond. Gang leaders kindly sort the issues between the opposite gang as soon as possible when it starts growing day by day then it will lead to a big issue.

- Every gang must have a name and it has to be registered with all the members of the gang. If both the gangs have spoken mutually they can do any kind of gang Rp as per their wish but make sure you don't exceed the gang members count of 12 members.

- There is no friendly gang Rp in Thamizhan Roleplay. Every gang should be dealing their problems only by themselves and other gangs should not help them in gang Rp.

- Gang members count can be minimum of 12 members and maximum of 25 members but in any gang fight or gang related role-play scenarios max of 12 members can engage. This Rule has to be strictly followed. Unregistered member cannot take part in any Gang activities.

- In your own gang territory or Operation base there can be more than 12 members to defend your area from police or gang attacks but it should be only the registered gang members.

- Gang fight should not be taken inside the green zones and business areas like hospitals, community service area, mechanic sheds, car dealers, bike dealers, boat dealers, real estate offices, logistics etc.

- Every gang when doing a gang related activity they are advised to wear a gang outfit and gang coloured car to differentiate between civilian.

- If you are engaging a scenario with a normal civilian then your team count cannot be more than 4 for that scenario.

- Gang scenarios or gang meetings or gang fight will be little bit toxic. Make sure you adapt to it before planning to do gang activities. This doesn't mean you can do an offensive and very bad toxic Rp which spoils the role-play fun.

- Using of vulgar or swear word is strictly prohibited especially female characters should be treated well misbehaving, sexual harassment or taking about dress or appearance to a female character will lead to immediate ban.

- Streamers are not allowed to talk vulgar or swear words about a player to your audience that will lead to toxic environment. Server action will be taken if any of the gang member or leader is found character discriminating or sexual harassment talks towards the opponent player on live in any social platform. If a player speaks bad in game using in-game mic or in live stream or other source this will be considered as verbal abuse and immediate ban or server action will be taken.

- Don't involve in gun fights for silly reasons. Try to speak out, have a gang meeting, and provide a good quality RP.

- Using of Double meaning words towards an opposite gang which are common in Usage which give a direct meaning of a Dirty word inside game through any in-game communication will lead to temporary ban or permanent ban based on severity.

- Unwanted emotes like Pee, middle finger or other vulgar emotes are strictly prohibited and this will lead to temporary or permanent ban based on severity.

- Robberies should not be done in gang dress. Will be strictly monitored by admins.

- Official gang named twitter accounts should be used for gang tweets to inform them about the attack to warn them. This can be done through phone calls also or by kidnaping a gang mate etc.

- If a gang members shows himself as a part of a particular gang in twitter or any IC communication platforms, police have the right to arrest him, he will be fired from whitelisted job, fined and jailed.

- Gang name, its members, leader name should be kept confidential. If it is known to the police in IC by proper roleplay, then police can issue warrant and arrest the members / leader of a gang.

- PD Attack should not be done for any gang related issues.

Gang Area Takeover rule :

- The attacking gang need to inform takeover In Discord 'Gang takeover' and then initite RP in IC.

- After announcement the takeover must happen within 1 week if not admins will decide for whom that place belongs. There is no certain rules to engage only gun fight for takeover, you rather can do some other RP like races, boxing etc if both gangs mutually accepts.(Using money is forbidden)

- In a Take over Gang war police should announce 144 in that area and police should not involve in the gang fight.

- If the holding gang or attacking gang is not available at the time of gang war and if the gang fails to start the war within 1 hour then the gang place will be the given to the available gang.

- The registered gang members only should attend the gang war , if the admins find that a person is a non-registered gang member then server action will be taken.

- If any of the gang members fail the rules then the gang leader and that gang member is responsible for his / hers mistake.

- After Takeover war that particular gang place will be in Cooldown for one week.

- If a gang member is down in a gang war then he / she should respawn

- If any gang member is found braking RP rule then the gang will get a black mark and server action will be taken based on rule break.

- In case of any problem like , crash , net problem , or power cuts then he or she should not come again in the fight .

- Before any official gang war, both Gang-Leaders must submit the list of official members (12 members) participating in that war in the particular gang chat without fail.

- Each gang can only have two gang places. If you want to take over another gang place, you must leave one of the gang places you own (if you have 2).

- Gang operation base will be requested only after 10 days from the date of registration.

- Gang operation bases will be only decided by the admins not by the gang.

- Takeover can't be initiated on a gang operation base here after.

- If a gang once successfully takeover an official place, the gang operation base of that particular gang will be removed.

- The defending gang who own the place will decide the weapons and drugs to use in the war if mutually accepted by both gangs in OOC.

- If attacking gang does not accept the defenders demand then any guns, drugs can be used (tazer and melee cannot be used)

- 6 cars can be used by both gangs and that should not be used to block the enterance

- No demands can be made on gang borders for gang war. border map will be available in 'gang location' text channel in discord.


BLUE BORDER : Gang control area RED BORDER : War Battle zone


- First the defending gang will be in the gang control area (blue) and the attacking gang can enter the battle zone.

- After attacking gang enter they can give a supress fire and after that defenders and attackers both can engage in fight within the battlezone and gang control area.

- War should end within 2 hours from the supress fire.

- After the war starts both the attackers and the defenders should not leave the marked zones if went unknowningly or due to ramming vechicle try to come inside as early as possible.

- Admins will monitor the war and if any rule break or any unfair fight take place they can cancel the war any time.

Gang Fight Cooldown:


- Individual Gang Fight Cool Down time is 3:00 hours.

- If a gang A has initiated a Gang fight with Gang B to initiate a gang RP and most of the people are down in Gang B, then Gang B can attack back in 30 min of cool down time for revenge. After this both team has to wait 3 hours for next fight.

- The above rule is for avoiding RDM. The brief explanation for the above line is just make sure one attack by gang A and one revenge attack by gang B can be performed and after this there should be a cool down of 3 hours.

Gang Colour And Gang Scenario:



- Every gang must register their gang vehicle colour and a gang outfit, only the registered Gang coloured cars and outfits should be used during gang scenarios. (Plan accordingly and initiate a Gang RP). If you have planned to change your outfit colour or car colour it has to be informed to Member of parliament before initiating any Gang Related RP.

- Registered Gang colours (Outfit and vehicles) should be used by the gangs during the gang scenario.

- If a gang/gang member has an IC knowledge about a person to which gang he/she represent, then the other gang/gang member can initiate gang RP with the opposite person / gang irrespective of the dress code or vehicle used by them with a proper IC reason.

If a person represents a gang and the opponent (Other Gang / Gang Members) have an IC knowledge, the person won't be considered as a normal civilian.

- Priority for colour will be given for the Gangs who registers first. For example, if a Gang named A has registered for Red coloured car and Red coloured outfit they can use any red dress for gang related activities. If another gang B has registered a car colour other than red and Gang Outfit Red in different pattern they are allowed but they are not allowed to use other red outfits like gang A. but Gang A cannot use the same red outfit which team B chose.

- Gangs can question the civilians and other gangs if they use a registered gang coloured car / registered outfit (can initiate gang RP).

- Civilians can use any dress even in same colour other than registered gang outfits.

- Civilians can use any registered gang coloured cars / registered outfit but on their own risk. Gang who own that coloured car/outfit have a complete right to question them and proceed with a Gang RP. But strictly no RDM.

- If a gang or gang member has an IC knowledge or information (Twitter, Ads, Etc.) of a person to which gang he/she belongs then they can proceed with a Gang RP with proper reason.

- If a gang planned to initiate a Gang RP on an opposite gang (Planned), they should initiate only in their gang outfit.

- If any gang member kidnapped as a hostage for robbery scenario / PD situation, after the scenario he/she should not initiate Gang RP for kidnapping him/her as a hostage unless they reveal their identity.

- If a gang member is kidnapped for robbery and if police has not responded for that triggered / non-triggered robbery the gang mates / friends of the hostage can recover him if they have an IC information through radio or phone (It's mandatory to use cuff and rope for kidnapping for robbery scenario) only 4 gang members can respond for that recovery RP. Kidnappers should also defend with 4 members.

Operation Base and Official Gang Place Rules:



- Gang operation base will be provided only after 10 days from the date of registration.
- Gang operation bases will be only decided by admins and not by the gang but they can give suggestions.
- Gang operation base will be provided to the registered gang if they don't have any official gang areas for their gang operation.
- Takeover can't be initiated on a gang operation base.
- In official gang place or operation base maximum of 25 gang official gang members can defend their own area. But attacking team should be only 12.
- Official Gang place owning gangs will be given some advantages like drug and other illegal jobs, etc.
- During any active scenario gang are not allowed to access their gang vaults in their gang place.
- If the gang fight is away from Gang area or gang operation base, then only 12 vs 12 fight should happen.
- Gang registration form or member update form will be opened after every 15 days only.
- Robbery scenario should not end in official gang place or operation bases.
