RULES

1) Be professional.
2) Follow traffic laws while patrolling.
3) Don't use lights and sirens unless responding to an emergency call.
4) Wear proper Medic related outfit while on duty.
5) Do not use Medic vehicle while off duty.
6) Do not commit any crimes, or aid in any crimes.(even if you are in off duty)
7) Alert police to any serious crimes you see being actively committed.
8) **You can carry only legal pistol.**
9) Evryone should use only Medic vechiles while on duty for attending cases.
10) Always lock your vechicle at all time.
11) All Medic Assitant must be accompanied by a doctor or assossiates unless given permission to ride solo prior to shift.
12) Refill your med-kits and bandages whenever possible and stay full.

Protocols

1) Treat all patients Carefelly each and every life is important.
2) Do not treat patients in roadways unless necessary, move all ambulatory patients to sidewalks as soon as possible.You can treat patients only on spot if u are carrying ambulance.
3) If you are responding to a scenario inform in your radio and move on.
4) Introduce yourself to the patient and get their information in return after you treated the preson.
5) Don't ever enter into a suspicious situation at any cost without informing to the police.
