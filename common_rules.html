Common Rules




You must have a working mic to play in Thamizhan roleplay

Usage of third-party application during in-game such as discord, other applications which gives you advantage inside game is strictly prohibited under any circumstances this may lead to temporary or permanent ban.

You must respect a player in and out of RP. Talking about race, gender, religion etc., will not be tolerated. Any form of harassment, disrespectful behaviour, using un-parliamentary words will result in severe consequences or permanent ban based on severity.

If a problem or issues faced needs to be resolved always use the chain of command. Make a ticket in discord and Speak to a helping hand first before speaking to PRO or MOP or Ministry.

Disrespecting or talking bad to helping hands or admins while handling OOC issues will lead to Permanent ban.

We appreciate your care and helping tendency about the community and server, but please leave it to our server staffs to deal with admin and OOC issue situations.

Steam or discord names that looks offensive will receive warning or ban. Everyone is requested to keep appropriate names. If you continue to join after being warned with an offensive name, a server removal/ban will be considered without further intimation.

Defaming our Thamizhan Roleplay server or individual player or a team in live streams, videos, inside game or outside game will receive temporary ban or permanent ban based on severity. Promoting other servers in-game or in any means or spamming the server members with unnecessary advertisements of any kind is strictly prohibited this will lead to permanent ban, Kindly respect others privacy and act accordingly.

If you witness someone breaking the rules first complete the Scenario properly and contact a Helping Hands in discord by raising a ticket. Please try to report in-game name/steam name of the defaulted person, what happened, and video proof if possible. After raising a ticket don't raise a problem again inside the game or in OOC. You are not allowed to talk about rules or fail RP inside game if spoken server action will be taken.

All the rules are subject to change based on the feedbacks and experience that will be decided by admins if required.

GREEN ZONES:


Green zones should be crime-free. No crime should be initiated from the green zone. It is Fail RP to run away from a crime and enter the green zone to avoid RP but police station is excluded from this rule. If the RP begins outside of it and is brought into the green zone, you may continue the criminal RP, however, it must be realistic. An example, in which it is not allowed is bringing a gang vs gang fight to the Hospital or Police station.

Hospital, Premium Dealership, Police Department (PD), all Business areas. community service location is not a safe zone but initiating a RP with a person doing community service or a person released from jail is considered as a fail RP.



Account Ownership and Responsibility

Account ownership is non-transferable, the property of the creator can't be shared or traded.

All players are responsible for their account, IP address, social club, and hardware. Linked accounts will face the same punishments and cannot be unlinked.



VALUE YOUR LIFE:


Fear-RP:

Fearing for your life as you do in a real life. For example, if you are alone in a place and 4 members hold you at gunpoint and you cannot escape, and you must cooperate you must follow Fear-RP. A person in such a situation would follow their demands as they are helpless. you wouldnâ€™t just walk away or quit or Try to avoid by acting as AFK or exploiting emote advantages. IF you are unarmed and a single person gun points very near to you then you should cooperate by raising hand. You cannot switch weapon and shoot. While raising hand you cannot use radio or other communication devices.



NO POWER-GAMING:


Power-gaming:

Using in-game or RP mechanics without allowing other players the freedom to act or defend themselves. This includes forcing RP on others, so they are obligated to lose.

In order to follow the power gaming rule, you must always use your /me command in a way that will give the other player the opportunity to resist, in the event your /me are directed towards other players. Example- If a police officer is near you and he is about to search you, you cannot transfer the items in your inventory to the nearby person or vehicle without using /me command (example /me transferring black money)



NO META-GAMING:


Meta-gaming:

The act of using out-of-character/external information for in-game situations. This includes stream sniping, 3rd party applications etc.



NO EXPLOITING OR HACKING:



No external apps, system supporting apps, hacking or cheating is allowed. This includes abusing in-game bugs, etc. This will result in an immediate server actions or permanent Ban.
If you find any bugs kindly report to helping hands or admins through discord if exploited that will lead to temporary or permanent ban.



NLR:(NEW LIFE RULES)



NLR (New-Life Rule): If your character is down and has received injuries that cannot be saved (e.g. Burnt, drowned in ocean for a long time etc.) you have to respawn. you will not have the memory of the cause of your death.
When a new life starts, your character will forget all the information and events leading up to your death. However, you are still the same character (you will have your same job, gang, friends).

You cannot return to the ongoing/current RP scenario if you have respawned or revived.



Letting Medic Know: (Using /me)



If You were killed in a way where you cannot be revived or burned, please let the medics know in RP. For example, /me was shot in the head multiple times and has no pulse, /me burned, /me committed suicide. Using /me to convey that you should not be revived is mandatory. Even if Medic fails to see that and revives you mistakenly, you should follow NLR (New Life Rule) mentioned above. /me should be using when medic is checking your body condition about your injury for example if you are down by accident and got injured in legs use /me injured in legs to cooperate EMS Roleplay.



When you are down:



You are allowed to send distress signal to Ems and You cannot communicate with anyone while you are down, especially not giving any information to your teammates. Usage of radio/3rd party communication for speaking after you are down is considered as Fail RP.



When you are revived:



If you have been revived, you will gain your memory in 2 mins disregard NLR. However, you shall RP your injury for at least 5 mins like trying to gain your memory or acting like you are injured in your head - this depends on the injury as well. If you are planning a Revenge RP after got revived, you have to wait for 30 OOC mins (Cool down Time). Also, the character should not go back to the same location for the cool down time mentioned above.

Your character remembers all the information before they were downed

Do not force respawn during RP situations. Remember you are still in RP. For example, If you are down in a robbery scenario and police is waiting for medic you should not force re-spawn as the scenario is not over yet. This will lead to ban



NO COMBAT LOGGING:



If you are in the middle of an RP scenario or if you're down in a fight do not log out this is considered as combat logging.
If your game crashes in the middle of a RP scenario you can continue the scenario by logging in again or inform in discord about it to the persons involved in the RP.

If you are caught by the police or in the middle of any scenario, your game crashes you have to continue the scenario by logging in again by contacting the concern person through OOC chat in-game. If not possible, notify in discord to concern department in OOC chat.
If you have initiated a scenario and just missed eyesight from police or civilian, you are not allowed to quit the game immediately you should wait at least 5 minutes in a hideout before storing the vehicle in the garage.



COPS/EMS



If there are only three cop/ems on duty, you are not supposed to kidnap the cop/ems.
HOWEVER, if a cop/ems put themselves in danger, this rule will not be applicable since it has happened due to their own fault.

No cop/EMS baiting:

Do not purposefully try and get police/EMS attention on you by doing things you would not do with an officer near you. You cannot constantly target the cop/ems when they are on duty.

Use of Player List Menu:

All citizens shall act like there are always cops present inside the city and do RP accordingly. Using Player List Menu information and take RP advantage is considered as fail RP and should be avoided.



Supress Fire or verbal warning about the action:



Killing a person without any Role-play initiated on a particular scenario is considered as RDM. Killing a person should be done only if there is a valid reason behind it. Supress fire is shooting in the air or in the ground without hitting the opposite party just to warn them that you are going to shoot them. Suppress fire should be used if you are not able to communicate and warn then verbally to give time for the opposite party to react whether to surrender or escape or to fight back. After a supress fire the opposite party must be given 5-10 secs time to take cover, surrender or to react. If you are not able to warn verbally then it is mandatory to use a suppress fire. This is applicable for both civilians. Even after a police chase in robbery also suppress fire rule to be followed.



Examples:


1. If you are in your gang place and person is using your gang garage for storing vehicle immediately you cannot shoot for using your gang garage. You can inform him that you should not use the garage if he cooperates and leave the area then there is no need of killing him or shooting him. If he refuses to obey your order, then you have to verbally warn him that I will shoot you if use our garage and you can give him a deadline like I will give to 20 secs to clear the area or I will shoot or you can. Even after this warning if he is still in the area then you may shoot him.

2. When you are in a on foot police chase and police see a weapon in your hand then they have to verbally communicate to you they will shoot or they can give supress fire before shooting at you.

killing/attempting to kill a citizen without any intent or roleplay. VDM (Vehicle death match): using a vehicle to kill/attempt to kill without any intent or roleplay.

An act in which a person uses his vehicle as a weapon in order to protect his own life from any direct or immediate threat if there is no other way, will not be considered VDM. But if he has space to take cover or escape the scenario and if he purposely does a VDM, server action shall be taken. or using a vehicle to block doors (i.e. a bank or a store)

/me Command usage:

/me is a role-play command used to describe actions your character performs

Examples

1. /me takes a handcuffs and attempts to handcuff him.

2. /me removing radio & gps from hostage

Do not use /me for showing your thoughts

Do not use /me to speak

/me command is something that is used to show an action or visible emotion of a character. Example if the character is upset, you can use '/me having upset look on face' or '/me sadness in face'
